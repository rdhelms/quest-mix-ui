Intro

Here you can provide information for players when they begin a quest in your world.

Custom

Here you can specify values that are relevant in many scenes throughout your world.

For example, if you set a value of "monsterLocation" to "house", then in a scene you could check the value of "monsterLocation" while setting its initial state or processing an event.

Any world event or scene event can access or change a world state variable.

World Events

Here you can handle events that may occur in your world.

Examples:

Randomly teleport the player's avatar every 5 minutes.

When player's score reaches 100, set world variable "danger" to "will robinson"

Scene Events

Here you can handle events that may occur in your scene.

Examples:

When player collides with (entity / object / collision-map-A), teleport to "Some Other Scene, (x,y)"

When player types (look|inspect|examine) & (tree|branches|up), display "You see a nest in the tree's branches."

When a player types (take) & (sword), display "You pick up the sword" and increase score by 5 points.

After 5 seconds, if "monsterLocation" is "house", change "Monster" entity's behavior to "chase".
